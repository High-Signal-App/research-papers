# Add Researcher Reading Buckets

## Why

The reading-path catalog should include the well-known Sutskever/Carmack reading list and adjacent researcher-backed paths without overloading the page navigation or risking copyrighted-content reuse.

## What

- Add source-attributed researcher reading buckets, including the public mirrors of the Sutskever/Carmack list.
- Keep ordering explicit inside each bucket.
- Rework site navigation into a compact list with dropdown groups.
- Add copyright-safe framing: links, metadata, and original briefs only; no copied abstracts or long excerpts.

## Out of scope

- Reader app integration.
- Full-text hosting or PDF mirroring.
- Corpus re-ingest.

## Verification

- Build the Astro app.
- Check for removed Reader integration strings.
- Check that the rendered navigation is compact on desktop and mobile.
